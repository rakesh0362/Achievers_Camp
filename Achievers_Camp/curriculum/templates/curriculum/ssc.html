Details of SSC Examination

Achievers Camp provides online classes for the SSC examination. You can join courses only for prelims or only for mains and if you are looking for a complete package, you can opt for the complete course of Bank.

What is SSC? SSC is an organization which conducts examination for Grade "B" and "C" for various ministries and departments for Government of India. Common Examination Conducted by SSC: SSC CGL (Combined Graduate Level) SSC CHSL (Combined Higher Secondary Level) SSC CPO (Central Police organization) SSC MTS (Multi-tasking Officer) SSC Stenographers
